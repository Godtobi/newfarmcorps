FarmCorps is a digital platform for rallying worldwide support for the causes of smallholder farmers especially in connecting them to both human and capital resources that empowers them to boost their farm production and income.

We are building a decentralised farm input supply chain and fund management platform to drive the transparent and efficient delivery of farm inputs by tokenizing funds giving by individuals, private organizations, local and international NGOs, inter-governmental organizations, and governments for the purchase of farm inputs for smallholder farmers and then tracking the effective use of these tokens for the purpose for which they are given

In 2019, We are running a pilot in partnership with Hub Afrique, Oyo State Agricultural Development Programme (OYSADEP) and International Institute of Tropical Agriculture (IITA) providing 500 women and youth smallholder farmers in Oke-Ogun, Oyo State with improved seeds, fertilizers, herbicides and extension services support.

Whether you are an individual or a large organization interested in the cause of smallholder farmers; you can click here to join our farmers support network to and see your efforts and donations impacting lives beyond.
