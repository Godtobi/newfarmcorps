What We Do

We, through our mobile platform, connect smallholder farmers to capital, agro-inputs, data-driven extension and markets using the contract farming business model. We provide farmers farming contracts from agro-processors & food manufacturers. The farming contracts are registered on distributed ledger (DLT) for a transparent process that enables farmers to use the contracts as asset to obtain capital for inputs, crop production from micro-finance or peer lenders.

Our Value Proposition

Farmers

Input Dealers

Lenders
